Mission Scope & Core Objective

Tasked with refining the integrity pipelines of next-generation multi-spectral imaging payloads. The focus centered on designing completely autonomous verification boundaries to scrub, validate, and benchmark massive raster layers streaming directly from remote-sensing products.

Engineering Contributions

  • Automated Validation Layers: Built end-to-end Python engine modules to scan incoming raw geo-spatial records, dropping pipeline visual exceptions instantly into strict handling queues.
  • Computer Vision Filtering: Leveraged advanced OpenCV structures to target, track, and flag data artifacts, ensuring clean spectral telemetry output profiles.
  • Geospatial Coordinate Mapping: Utilized the GDAL/OGR translation library to align coordinate spaces and ensure clean geospatial data consistency across deep telemetry maps.
